Ever opened a legacy project and wanted to burn it all down after ten minutes? Picked Serverless because it "scales" and then spent a weekend debugging cold starts? Written code that works but you already know you won't understand six months from now?

If at least one of these made you smile (or cry), let's take some time to talk about it together.

In this AperiDojo live stream, Christian Nastasi and Emiliano Pisu host Luciano Mammino, Senior Architect at fourTheorem, co-author of "Node.js Design Patterns", and maintainer of Middy, for an open conversation covering:

- **Practical Design Patterns**: which ones actually save projects
- **Serverless without the hype**: when it makes sense and when it doesn't
- **Node.js vs Rust**: how to choose without overthinking it
- **Real-world Open Source**: what it actually means to maintain Middy, used by thousands of developers
- **Filtering signal from noise** in the tech world

This is not a lecture — it's an open conversation. Bring your questions, share your experience, and join the discussion.

Grab your favourite drink and join us!
