In this live session with Francesco Ciulla, we explore the powerful combination of AWS Lambda and Rust.

We start by breaking down the "how, when, and why" of Lambda, discussing the core benefits of serverless architectures. The focus then shifts to why Rust is becoming a top choice for high-performance serverless functions, specifically looking at its ability to minimize cold starts and reduce infrastructure costs.

In the second part, I join the stream to lead a live technical demo, showing how to build and deploy a Rust-based Lambda function in real-time.

If you want to learn more about building Lambda functions in Rust, check out [rust-lambda.com](https://rust-lambda.com/).
